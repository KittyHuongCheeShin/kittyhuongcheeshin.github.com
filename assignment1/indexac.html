Typically Swiss Hotels

Typically Swiss hotels combine regional architecture with a genuine Swiss atmosphere and are as diverse as Switzerland itself. But all of them - from city palaces and country inns to Alpine summer houses - are characterised by their hosts, offering local specialities and an authentic Swiss holiday experience.
